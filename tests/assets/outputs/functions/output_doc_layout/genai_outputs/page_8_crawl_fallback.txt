Schedule 1 Amendment of Road Transport Act 2013 No 18
[1] Whole Act
Omit all bracketed notes wherever occurring in headings that draw attention (“cf”) to
provisions of other Acts and statutory rules.
[2] Section 4 Definitions
Omit the definitions of alcohol or other drug related driving offence  and fatal accident
from section 4(1). 
Insert in alphabetical order—
alcohol or drug related driving offence  means an offence—
(a) involving the presence of—
(i) alcohol in a person’s breath or blood, or
(ii) a drug in a person’s oral fluid, blood or urine, and
(b) prescribed by the statutory rules.
bicycle  has the same meaning as in the Road Rules 2014 .
crash  means—
(a) a collision between 2 or more vehicles, or
(b) a collision or incident involving a vehicle in which—
(i) a person is killed or injured, or
(ii) the vehicle or other property is damaged, or
(iii) an animal in a person’s charge is killed or injured.
fatal crash  means a crash on a road or road related area involving a motor
vehicle or bicycle that results in the death of 1 or more persons.
