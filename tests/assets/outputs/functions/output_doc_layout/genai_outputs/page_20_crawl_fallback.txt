Schedule 1   Amendment of Road Transport Act 2013 No 18
Validity of certificates
(1) A certificate under Schedule 3, Part 5, Division 2 must not be considered
invalid if the certificate would have been valid had the amendments made tothat division by the amending Act not been made.
(2) This clause ceases to have effect 2 years after the commencement of this
clause.1
